## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**ExpenseFlow – Your Personal Finance Manager, 100% Private**

Take complete control of your finances with ExpenseFlow — a beautifully designed expense tracker that keeps ALL your data on YOUR device. No cloud, no subscriptions, no data sharing. Ever.

**🔒 100% Private — Your Data Never Leaves Your Device**
Everything you track — transactions, accounts, budgets, investments — is stored locally on your phone. We don't have servers. We literally cannot see your data.

**📊 Complete Financial Overview**
• Dashboard with income, expenses, savings rate and net worth
• Beautiful charts — monthly trends, category breakdown, spending patterns
• Recent transactions at a glance

**💳 Track Everything**
• Bank Accounts — savings, salary, current, NRE/NRO, wallets, cash
• Credit Cards — outstanding balance, credit utilization, expiry tracking
• Subscriptions — Netflix, Spotify, gym — never miss a renewal
• Investments — Mutual funds (SIP tracking) + NSE/BSE stocks with live prices

**📷 Smart Receipt Scanner**
Point your camera at any bill or receipt. Our on-device AI reads it and fills in the transaction automatically. No photo is ever uploaded.

**🏦 Bank Statement Import**
Upload your ICICI Bank PDF statement and all transactions import automatically — no manual entry needed.

**📋 Budget Tracking**
Set monthly limits by category. Get instant alerts when you're 80% or 100% over budget.

**📤 Export & Backup**
Export all your data as JSON backup anytime. Import it on a new phone in seconds.

**✈️ Works Offline**
Full functionality without internet. Stock prices are the only feature that needs connectivity.

## App Store Review Notes (for Apple reviewer)
This app stores all user data locally using device storage (localStorage/Capacitor Preferences).
No network requests are made except:
1. Yahoo Finance API (stock prices) — only stock symbols, no personal data
2. CORS proxy for Yahoo Finance (corsproxy.io) — only stock symbols

Camera permission is used for the Receipt Scanner feature which performs OCR on-device using Tesseract.js WebAssembly. No images are uploaded.

Test the app without an account — data is created on first launch.
